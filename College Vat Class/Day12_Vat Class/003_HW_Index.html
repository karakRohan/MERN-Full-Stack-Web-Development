Virat Kohli Biography

Virat Kohli is one of the most celebrated cricketers in the world,
known for his aggressive batting
style, leadership qualities,
and consistent performance on the international stage.
Here's a detailed
biography:
Early Life and Background
Full Name: Virat Kohli
Date of Birth: November 5, 1988
Place of Birth: Delhi, India
Family:
Father: Prem Kohli (a criminal lawyer)
Mother: Saroj Kohli (a homemaker)
Siblings: An elder brother, Vikas Kohli, and an elder sister, Bhavna Kohli
Kohli developed an early interest in cricket,
beginning his training at the West Delhi Cricket
Academy when he was just nine years old. His natural
talent and dedication were evident from a young age,
and he quickly rose through the ranks of junior cricket.
